Mission, vision, approach

Curate GRCA Ltd is a Kenyan firm working on risk, governance, compliance, and analytics. Our clients are mostly banks, with some insurers, corporates, and public-sector institutions across East Africa.

MISSION

Help our clients build risk management that holds up under real conditions — not just in the policy document.

VISION

Be the firm African banks call first when the risk question is hard.
