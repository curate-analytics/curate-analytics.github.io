§IV

What FLOWS does

№ 01

Interest-rate risk

Repricing gaps, EaR, EVE sensitivity, parallel and non-parallel scenarios.

№ 02

Liquidity risk

LCR, NSFR, LAR — daily, weekly, ad-hoc — with stress overlays.

№ 03

Balance sheet

Position concentrations, behavioural NMD, funding mix.

№ 04

Stress testing

Run-on-deposits, deposit concentration, market-wide funding stress, systemic crisis.

№ 05

Dashboards

KPI tiles for ALCO. Thresholds, traffic-light status, drill-down to the cell.

№ 06

Regulatory reports

ICAAP and ILAAP returns in the format your regulator asks for.

§V

How it works

01

Bring your model

Upload your existing Excel risk model. We parse it once, keeping every formula and dependency. Nothing is rewritten.

02

Add the period data

Each reporting period, your analysts upload the inputs — balances, rates, segments. The engine recomputes the whole formula graph.

03

Get the numbers out

Computed cells become KPIs, ALCO dashboards, and the regulator return. Every input, formula, and result is logged.
